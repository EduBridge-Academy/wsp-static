Work Simulation Program Tracks

Develop employability skills by taking up real life projects.



Industry Analysis (Consulting)



Track Overview

Learning industry analysis and understanding how value chains work. You will understudy or lead consultants, and make recommendations for growth.


Objectives

In this track you’ll learn
  1. Industry Dynamics
  2. Business Negotiation
  3. Advisory Design

Company Research (Investment Research)



Track Overview

Together, we’ll demystify financial reports and advise partners on key investment recommendations.


Objectives

At the end of this track, you should be able to:
  1. Understand how to find key details in financial statements.
  2. Use financial information to value and analyze firms.
  3. Provide investment advice to firms.

Data Analytics



Track Overview

We’ll help you make sense of patterns and become a business prophet. You’ll help companies predict the future and identify patterns that can help them grow using data.


Objectives

At the end of this track, you should be able to:
  1. Write analytics proposals to firms.
  2. Use data analytics to improve operational efficiency.
  3. Translate raw data to digestible business stories.

Human Resources



Overview

You’ll learn how to help companies identify, groom and retain superstar talents.


Objectives

You'll learn:
  1. Employee management
  2. Talent attraction
  3. Resource training
  4. Employment negotiations
  5. Talent appraisal
  6. Compensation strategies

Accounting



Track Overview

Get to compile and prepare detailed financial information for firms.


Objectives

This course will give you a practical understanding of:
  1. Management accounting
  2. Corporate finance strategies
  3. The relationship between accounting and microeconomics.
