What is Work
Simulation Programme

This program is how recent graduates and penultimate students build work experience without leaving their homes. We design simulations that replicate everyday work challenges for remote engagement. What’s the topping? Employers get to find ready to employ talents through us.

How will this improve
my skills & experience

You will get to do proper work and apply knowledge to real life scenarios. Yes, it’s a simulation but your work will impact the decision of firms you intern remotely with. Also, you get to add your time with us as an actual work experience on your resume.

Who can join & what
should I expect

The program is open to final or penultimate students, recent graduates, corp members and job seekers looking to boost their resumes. Expect to enjoy global exposure to workplace development, and build a star studded resume.





I don’t see my interested
career. What do I do?

Your experience with us is well rounded to help you build skills relevant to any career. So, you have nothing to worry about.
